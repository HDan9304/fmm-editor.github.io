Clubs CSV → JSON Maker

Paste or upload CSV, convert to clubs.json, download, and replace your file in the repo.

Quick guide

  1. CSV can be with or without header. If no header, we assume common, official, logo, aliases.
  2. Click Convert → JSON. (Optional) tick auto-fetch logos to fill empty logo cells.
  3. Click Download clubs.json and put it next to your HTML.
Header optional. Default order: common, official, logo, aliases
